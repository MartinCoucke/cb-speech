Central bank money for the digital era (2026-06-25)
ECB Executive Board member Piero Cipollone addressed the International Banking Federation on the Eurosystem's comprehensive payments strategy, focusing on the digital euro for retail use, tokenised central bank money for wholesale transactions (Pontes and Appia initiatives), and cross-border payment improvements. The speech outlined timelines including a digital euro pilot starting in the second half of 2027 and a potential first issuance in 2029. No discussion of interest rates, inflation, or monetary policy stance was included.
